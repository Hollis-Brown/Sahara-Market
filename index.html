Indulge in rich, smooth chocolates that combine the finest ingredients with a touch of Arabian luxury.

Meat Products

From tender lamb to savory chicken, Mediterranean meat products are known for their bold flavors and high quality.

Canned & Jarred Goods

Mediterranean canned and jarred goods preserve the vibrant flavors of fruits, vegetables, and sauces for year-round enjoyment.

Dried Fruits & Nuts

A popular Mediterranean snack, dried fruits and nuts offer a sweet and crunchy mix full of nutrients and natural energy.

Cookies & Wafers

Light, crisp cookies and wafers are perfect for satisfying a sweet tooth, often flavored with nuts, chocolate, or fruit.

Bakery & Pastries

Mediterranean bakeries are famous for their sweet treats like baklava, made with layers of flaky pastry, honey, and nuts, as well as other delicious pastries and savory breads often flavored with olive oil and herbs.

Confectionary

Mediterranean confectionary delights include a variety of sweet treats made with honey, nuts, and exotic spices.

Dairy

Creamy cheeses, yogurt, and milk products are staples in Mediterranean cuisine, offering both richness and nutrition.
